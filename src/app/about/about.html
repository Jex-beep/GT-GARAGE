The owner

Honest work,
no surprises

Glen started GT Garage because he saw too many customers getting talked into repairs they didn't need. Cars are expensive enough without a shop padding the bill.

The rule here is simple: diagnose first, explain what was found in plain terms, and only do the work that actually needs doing. If it can wait, he'll tell you that too.

  • Proper diagnostics before every repair
  • Clear pricing, explained up front
  • All makes and models welcome
  • Work we'd be proud to do on our own cars
